Bio

I'm a product designer and creative technologist based in New York, currently pursuing an MFA in Design & Technology at Parsons School of Design.

I work at the intersection of product, culture, and emerging technology: AR exhibitions, VR narratives, enterprise UX, and brand systems. What ties it together is a consistent interest in how design shapes the way people feel and understand the world around them.

Before Parsons, I worked with EyeJack, Deloitte Digital, and Philips, and have had work recognised at the Kyoorius Design Awards and MIT Reality Hack.
