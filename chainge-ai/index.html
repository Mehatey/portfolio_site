01 Concept

What if AI was a presence
in your life?

Chainge AI imagines an assistant through mixed reality that connects small moments across your day. Not a tool you open, but a presence that observes, reflects, and gently surfaces what matters.

Most people lack reflection and support, not information. The project explores what an AI companion looks like when it prioritizes awareness over productivity.

02 Design

The line between help
and intrusion.

The hardest design challenge was defining when the AI should speak and when it should stay silent. Every intervention needed to feel like a suggestion, never a demand.

The interface is ambient — spatial cues in mixed reality that appear at the edges of attention rather than the center. Presence without pressure.

03 Insight

People lack reflection

Most people lack reflection and support, not information. When AI shifts from answering questions to noticing patterns, it becomes something closer to a companion than a search engine.
