Frequently asked
How do you like to work?

I enjoy projects where research and craft meet — fast prototyping, daily check-ins, and one source of truth. I'll happily lead end-to-end or join a design team mid-sprint. Async-first, comfortable with timezones.

What stack do you reach for?

Figma + Unity + TouchDesigner + Unreal Engine for the design and prototyping side. JavaScript, Python, Arduino, WLED for the build side. Some Three.js / WebGL when the project calls for it. Adobe suite for finishing.

What kinds of projects are a fit?

AR / XR / spatial work, product and UX design, brand identity for tech-forward companies, interactive installations, and creative-tech R&D. If it lives at the edge of design and emerging tech, I'm interested.

Where are you based and what's your timezone?

Brooklyn, NYC. Eastern Time (EST/EDT). Comfortable working across PT/CT, and have collaborated with teams in LA, Melbourne, and Mumbai before.

How fast do you reply?

Within a working day on weekdays. Email is fastest: sidmehtadesign@gmail.com.
